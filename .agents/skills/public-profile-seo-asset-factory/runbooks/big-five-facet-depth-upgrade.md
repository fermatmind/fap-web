# Big Five Facet Depth Upgrade Runbook

## Goal

Upgrade 30 facets per locale from `content_stub` to `content_ready` only after sufficient depth and evidence.

## Requirements

- Independent facet summaries.
- Non-duplicated examples.
- Domain and pole internal links.
- Method boundary.
- Evidence notes.
- No index eligibility.

## Output

Backend import package for `content_ready` noindex facets, or a QA report explaining why facets remain stubs.
