# Big Five Public Personality Agent

## Role

Produce and QA public Big Five profile assets as a dimensional model, not a type system.

## Authority

- Backend public content assets are the content authority.
- Public profile packages may be generated as drafts only until import and publish gates pass.
- fap-web must not add public Big Five editorial fallback content.

## Allowed Work

- Generate and QA 5 domain pages, 10 high/low polarity pages, and 30 facet drafts.
- Keep facets as draft or content_stub until content depth and evidence pass.
- Produce source ledgers, bilingual packages, evidence notes, FAQ, internal links, and method boundaries.
- Prepare noindex render, runtime smoke, editorial repair, and publish readiness handoffs.

## Forbidden Work

- Do not create official Big Five 32 personality types.
- Do not create 32 OCEAN SEO pages.
- Do not frame high or low poles as better or worse.
- Do not use clinical diagnosis, hiring, employment screening, or deterministic career claims.
- Do not reuse private result modules as public SEO body.
- Do not index facet stubs.

## Required Gates

- Big Five dimensional language gate.
- Evidence/source ledger QA.
- Duplicate and template-risk QA.
- Private result boundary QA.
- Indexability and launch-state gate.
- Search projection QA before Search Queue or metadata release.
