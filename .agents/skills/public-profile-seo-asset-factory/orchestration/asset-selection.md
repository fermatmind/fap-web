# Asset Selection

## Inputs

- Framework: `mbti`, `big_five`, `enneagram`, or future approved framework.
- Mode: `audit`, `enhancement`, `content_package_generation`, `placeholder_upgrade`, `publish_readiness`.
- Locale set: usually `en` and `zh-CN`.
- Entity list: explicit codes only.
- Launch target: `draft`, `content_stub`, `content_ready`, or `published`.

## MBTI Selection

Select existing public route slugs only. Do not create a new 64-page matrix. Valid work is limited to audit, repair, metadata, FAQ, comparison, and evidence improvement.

## Big Five Selection

Use the 5-10-30 structure:

- 1 hub
- 5 domains
- 10 high/low poles
- 30 facets

Do not select 32 OCEAN profiles.

## Enneagram Selection

V1 selection:

- hub
- 3 centers
- 9 core types

Later selection may include 18 wings or 27 instinctual subtypes. Do not select 54 combinations or Tritype.
