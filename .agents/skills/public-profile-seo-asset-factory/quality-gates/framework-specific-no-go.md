# Framework-Specific No-Go

## MBTI

- Do not regenerate 64 A/T pages.
- Do not say A/T is official MBTI.
- Do not replace `PersonalityController` or CMS authority.

## Big Five

- Big Five is dimensional, not a fixed type model.
- Do not generate official 32 types.
- Do not create 32 OCEAN SEO pages.
- High/low poles are not good/bad judgments.
- Facet stubs must not index.

## Enneagram

- Start with 3 centers and 9 core types.
- 18 wings are later.
- 27 instinctual subtypes are later.
- Do not create 54 wing x instinct pages.
- Do not create Tritype.
- Do not frame Enneagram as clinical diagnosis or hiring basis.
