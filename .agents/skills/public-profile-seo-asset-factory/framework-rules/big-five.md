# Big Five Rules

## Mode

Use `content_package_generation`.

## Model

Big Five is a dimensional model. Use 5 domains, 10 high/low poles, and 30 facets.

## Allowed

- 34 V1 noindex render candidate content packages.
- Facet depth upgrade from `content_stub` to `content_ready` after QA.
- Publish readiness packet after explicit approval.

## Forbidden

- Official 32 personality types.
- 32 OCEAN SEO pages.
- Good/bad framing of high/low poles.
- Indexing facet stubs.
- Reusing Big Five private result modules as public SEO body.
