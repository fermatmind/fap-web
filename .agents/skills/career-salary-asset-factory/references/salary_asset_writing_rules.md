# Salary Asset Writing Rules

Generate reader-facing assets only after evidence and estimate ledgers both pass audit.

Every asset must:

- Cite only evidence IDs and sources present in the PASS evidence ledger.
- Use salary numbers only from the PASS estimate ledger.
- Preserve China recruitment boundary.
- Preserve US mapping, aggregate, military-only, and percentile limitations.
- Preserve UK direct/adjacent/variable-pay boundaries.
- Keep EU as macro context unless occupation-specific evidence exists.

Each asset needs career-specific salary drivers and reader guidance. Avoid template-only prose.

Never promise income, employment, certification success, promotion, immigration outcome, or personal salary result.
