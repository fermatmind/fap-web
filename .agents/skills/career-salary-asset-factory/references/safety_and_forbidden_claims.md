# Safety And Forbidden Claims

Forbidden claims:

- China official single-occupation wage when only recruitment evidence exists.
- Official median wage without official source.
- Personal income prediction.
- Guaranteed employment, promotion, certification, immigration, or salary outcome.
- Unsupported wage percentile, sample count, source year, job outlook, or annual openings.
- EU-wide occupation median salary from macro context.
- UK variable-pay profile converted into fixed salary.
- Civilian wage rows for military-only occupations.

When a source is weak, say it is weak in the evidence/estimate boundary; do not hide weakness in reader-facing text.
