# Invalid Proxy Rules

Use adjacent roles only when the duties, employer context, skills or license, and pay mechanism are close enough to support a bounded estimate.

Hard invalid examples:

- Air traffic controllers: do not use visa specialists, metro operations, airport admin, ground service, flight attendants, pilots, or generic airport customer service.
- Animal caretakers: do not use veterinarians, pet doctors, or veterinary medicine as the main proxy.
- Civil engineers: do not use weak-current or low-voltage engineers.
- Advertising sales agents: do not use sales admin supervisors.
- Aircraft mechanics: do not use aerospace engineering technicians as the main proxy.
- Waiters: do not use cashiers, baristas, chefs, or store managers as the main proxy.
- Wholesale/manufacturing sales reps: do not use sales admin supervisors.
- Agricultural workers: do not use agricultural technicians or agricultural engineers as a generic farm worker proxy.
- Music therapists: do not use ordinary music teachers or generic counseling as the main proxy.
- Military aviation and air crew roles: do not use airport admin, ground customer service, or generic engineers as salary proxy.
- Physicians and clinicians: do not use non-clinical admin roles as pay proxy.
- Teachers: do not use education operations, curriculum sales, or generic training sales as teacher salary proxy.

If only broad adjacent evidence exists, mark it low confidence, explain the boundary, and keep `ready_for_asset_generation=false` unless the audit explicitly accepts it.
