# Career Risk Future Source Rules

Prefer task-level AI/automation research, O*NET task composition, official safety/regulatory sources, BLS outlook, and industry cycle evidence.

Every source item must include source name, URL or citation handle, captured fact, source boundary, and whether the source is direct, aggregate, adjacent, or macro context.

For AI impact, also read `ai_source_rules.md`. AI source items must distinguish task taxonomy, external calibration, internal rubric, official profile, and macro context. Internal rubric evidence may support scoring but cannot be the only evidence for an occupation-level AI score.
