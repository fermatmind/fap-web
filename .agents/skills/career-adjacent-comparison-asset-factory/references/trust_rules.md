# Career Adjacent Comparison Trust Rules

Block title-similarity-only adjacency, salary-only adjacency, unrelated title collisions, and easy-transition promises.

Trust audit must fail rows with fabricated facts, unsupported proxy use, raw internal metadata, missing source boundaries, or reader-facing claims that exceed the evidence.

For graph-candidate readiness, the trust audit must also fail rows where:

- SOC/O*NET broad family is the only relationship proof.
- Work overlap exists but skill overlap is missing, or skill overlap exists but work overlap is missing.
- Relationship type, transfer cost, or evidence confidence is omitted.
- Regulated, safety-sensitive, clinical, legal, aviation, military, education, or public-authority roles lack a concrete boundary for license, supervision, jurisdiction, safety, accountability, or training.
