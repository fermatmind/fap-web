## AEO Answer-Block Rules

- Use direct-answer summaries only when the statement is visible on the page or backed by backend/CMS authority.
- Keep form, report, and free/full-result language inside the source-authority boundary recorded by the readiness and claim packets.
- Keep methodology, non-diagnostic, and non-deterministic boundaries visible for personality, career, IQ, and EQ claims.
- Do not add hidden FAQPage, hidden answer blocks, or schema-only claims.
- Do not create publishable CMS copy, article copy, result-page copy, or frontend fallback copy in this packet.

## Internal-Link Rules

- Preserve observed take-action, topic, personality, and career bridge links as evidence only.
- Do not widen sitemap, llms, canonical, hreflang, robots, schema, or indexability exposure.
- Do not add cross-hub or AEO amplification links until claim/copy gates pass.
- Do not infer graph, recommendation, personality-profile, or career outcome authority from frontend link presence.
